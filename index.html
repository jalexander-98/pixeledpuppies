The 'Pixeled Puppies' NFT collection consists of 2500 ERC-1155 collectible pixel-art puppies tokens with various major and minor traits. 

About Pixeled Puppies

A pixeled puppy is a non-fungible token (NFT) which is based on the ERC-1155 standard. This means that every token is completely non fungible and has different values due to it's visual. There will be only 2500 unique Pixeled Puppies, which are digitally scarce through the use of blockchain technology. All pixeled puppies are generated algorithmically to ensure that no two visuals are exactly alike. But there are still different probabilities for different traits which are listed below.
